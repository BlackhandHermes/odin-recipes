Fried Rice

This is a Fried Rice recipe I have used for the past five years as a base. Fried Rice is a form of base food, where ingriedients can change based on whats sitting in your fridge. You do not need a wok, but a pan that can very evenly distributed heat like cast iron will work very well. It also pays to chop things ahead of time, so one can add ingridients at a good time to ensure crispiness. I reccommend brunoice cut(dicing into cubes, doesnt have to be precise) for most of the vegatables, outside of the ones specified otherwise

Ingriedients

  • Day Old white rice or brown rice, cool not warm.-1 cup
  • Soy Sauce-2 oz or 5 to 6 tablespoons
  • One Carrot
  • Half of one onion or a bunch of spring onions
  • One celery stalk
  • 2 table spoons brown sugar or honey
  • One egg
  • two garlic cloves minced
  • 3 snack peppers or one whole bell pepper
  • toasted or regular sesame oil- 2 tablespoons
  • ginger, grated around 2 tablespoons or minced
  • one teaspoon black pepper
  1. Begin by heating up a pan to medium heat
