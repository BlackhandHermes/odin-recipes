Honey Garlic Chicken

This is recipe I have personally developed over time after seeing it on a budget youtube channel. This is a dish that is flavorful, pretty cheap and goes well with rice. Take your time and remember, its hard to overcook chicken thigh!

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken thighs, bone in or boneless, skin on or off.
  • Soy sauce, 6 to 7 tablespoons
  • Garlic well minced,nearly paste like 4 cloves
  • Ginger, well minced,2 tablespoons worth
  • Honey-4 tablespoons
  • Flour, or Corn starch(preferred)
  • Spring onion- one bunch
  • Oil, corn or vegetable 2 to 3 tablespoons enough to cover bottm of pan

Instructions

  1. Begin by heating up a pan with some oil, to medium high heat, and place your chicken skin side down for four to five minutes until crispy.
  2. Take your soy sauce, garlic, ginger,honey, white ends of the spring onions and give it a whisk or stir well. Pour into pan and reduce heat to low. reduce and baste and flip chicken every 5 mins for 20 mins.
  3. Chop green ends of your spring onions, and serve as a garnish with your chicken.
  4. Serve and Enjoy!
