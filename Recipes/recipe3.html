Mushroom Soup

This is a mushroom soup I have made for my family several times. I used to call it scrap soup because I used to clear out the fridge of older veggies and wilty mushrooms. After some time, we upgraded it to have a better flavor and more consistent ingriedients. One can even add cream if one desires a different taste profile. Soup is somethign you want to create in layers, to maximize flavors and textures. Do it slow, and watch as your soup turns to deliciousness!

Ingredients

  • Shitake Mushrooms, 12 ounces
  • Sweet or White onion, Large,One
  • One large carrot or several small ones.
  • One Stalk of celery
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • Broth, Chicken, 4 quarts worth, or bullion two cubes dissolved in water
  • 2 spoonfuls of honey
  • Parsley, one bunch
  • Thyme, one bunch
